Quote-Ability

Built by Lannon Khau

Quote-Ability is your personal literary companion. Upload a snapshot of your book and enter interactive AI-powered rooms that match your current reading progress. Whether you're exploring character arcs or saving your favorite quotes, this app turns every reading session into an unforgettable conversation.

📚 Coming Soon 📝 Join the Early Access

📖 What Quote-Ability Does

  • Visual Bookmarking: Snap a photo of your page, and the app finds your exact spot in the ebook.
  • AI Reading Room: Have live, spoiler-free discussions with an agent trained only on your current progress.
  • Book Mastermind: Talk to a full-book-trained assistant that can explain themes, plotlines, and answer your toughest literary questions.
  • Quote Curator: Save and organize your favorite lines across books, and chat with an agent who remembers them all.

🧠 Envisioned Tech Stack

  • Python OCR (Tesseract) for visual bookmarking
  • Flask backend with Streamlit or Discord frontend
  • OpenAI Assistants trained on uploaded EPUB/Book content
  • PostgreSQL or RDS for quote tracking & metadata
  • LangChain + Embedding search for contextual discussion
  • TailwindCSS for minimalist reading UI

💬 Why It Matters

We often read to remember, but how much do we truly retain? Quote-Ability ensures that your reading journey never fades. With AI-powered conversation, visual bookmarking, and curated quote recall, this app brings depth, context, and memory to your literary experience.

🛤 Roadmap

✅ Idea Validated: Real conversations with readers who crave deeper recall and engagement

🔎 R&D Phase: OCR testing, quote extraction, and non-spoiler text alignment

🧠 Assistant Training: Custom agents per book chapter, structured memory design

⚙️ MVP Design: Streamlit vs Discord – platform decision and initial deployment
