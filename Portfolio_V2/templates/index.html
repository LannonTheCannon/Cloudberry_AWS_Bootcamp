About Me

I ’m Lannon Khau, a full-stack engineer with a passion for building AI-powered systems that solve real problems and spark curiosity.

My journey started with a desire to build and evolved into an obsession with integrating AI to create smarter, more human tools. Along the way, I’ve learned that great systems don’t just function, they listen, adapt, and invite us to explore ideas we never knew we had.

Engineer. Builder. Explorer.

I believe in building systems that capture human intelligence through visual mind mapping.
