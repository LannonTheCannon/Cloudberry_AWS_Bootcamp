About Me

I ’m Lannon Khau, a full-stack engineer with a passion for building AI-powered systems that solve real problems and spark curiosity.

My journey started with a desire to build and evolved into an obsession with integrating AI to create smarter, more human tools. Along the way, I’ve learned that great systems don’t just function, they listen, adapt, and invite us to explore ideas we never knew we had.

Engineer. Builder. Explorer.

I believe in building systems that capture human intelligence through visual mind mapping. By learning from behavior and real-time patterns, these systems can continually train AI to make more informed, context-aware decisions and not just outputs based on static training data. When we align AI with human intent, its responses become more focused, adaptive, and meaningful.

“I build AI-powered systems that explore the unknown and turn raw data into insight, clarity, and creative tools.”
