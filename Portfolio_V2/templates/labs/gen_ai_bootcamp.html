T his bootcamp wasn’t just about building apps — it was about building intelligence. Matt Dancho and Business Science guided us through the real-world use of AI in marketing, forecasting, vector databases, and app deployment.

What We Built

From RAG marketing copilots to forecasting agents running on AWS Lambda, every clinic delivered an end-to-end lesson in production AI systems. We deployed real apps, engineered LLM chains, and made agents that think and respond like product teammates.

Technologies included Streamlit, AWS Bedrock, LangChain, vector databases (like FAISS and Pinecone), OpenAI fine-tuning, and tool calling — all tied together with production strategies like system design and CI/CD.

Why This Bootcamp Mattered

It gave me clarity on how to deliver value as a data scientist, not just write code. The biggest insight? It's not the algorithm that matters—it's the system you build around it, and how fast you iterate.

"In AI engineering, it's not about the model. It's about momentum."
